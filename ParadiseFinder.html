Explore the top four travel destinations of the month



Cape Town - South Africa

Endless winelands, an iconic mountain skyline and paradisiacal beaches. This is what you are going to discover in the South African capitol. Do not miss out on the car ride down.

Table Mountain

Cape Town is home to one of the New 7 Wonders of Nature - the Table Mountain. Even if you are a passionate hiker and want to explore the mountain within two hours by foot, make sure to make your return in the car to enjoy the breathtaking views. If you are an early bird take the first car up at 7:30 to enjoy the first rays and a tourist-free view. Explore it

Boulders Beach

Penguins and hot weather do not fit together? You are wrong. Not far from the city centre you can take a swim in the warm sea water while observing thousands of African penguins on the beach.

Ideal Travel Time

Visit Cape Town from December to February.

Paros - Greece

This island offers more than just scenic beaches and traditional greek villages. Join for some watersports like windsurfing or waterskiing to complete your holiday.

Nassau - Bahamas

Did you always want to swim with the cute pigs from the Bahamas? This and way more dreams can become true once you decide to book a flight to this exciting destination.
