Explore the top three travel destinations of the month



Cape Town - South Africa

Endless winelands, an iconic mountain skyline and paradisiacal beaches. This is what you are going to discover in the South African capitol. Do not miss out on the car ride down.

Table Mountain

Cape Town is home to one of the New 7 Wonders of Nature - the Table Mountain. Even if you are a passionate hiker and want to explore the mountain within two hours by foot, make sure to make your return in the car to enjoy the breathtaking views. If you are an early bird take the first car up at 7:30 to enjoy the first rays and a tourist-free view.

Boulders Beach

Penguins and hot weather do not fit together? You are wrong. Not far from the city centre you can take a swim in the warm sea water while observing thousands of African penguins on the beach.

Ideal Travel Time

Visit Cape Town from December to February, the best time for swimming and sightseeing.

Paros - Greece

This island offers more than "just" scenic beaches and traditional greek villages. Join for some watersports like windsurfing or waterskiing to complete your holiday.

Old Port Naoussa

If you want to experience the greek island life and relax in the sun the Old Port of Naoussa is going to be the right place for you. Visit the promenade filled with traditional tavernas and the narrow streets of the village to feel like a true local. Buy fish directly from a fishing boat or visit one of the many small boutiques for some unique souvenirs.

Pounta

If you look for some action during your holidays then look no more. This beach is the right place for some kitesurfing, diving, windsurfing and many other watersports. And you might even make it to the next island Antiparos.

Ideal Travel Time

Visit Greece from September to November to enjoy a warm holiday without crowded places.

Nassau - Bahamas

Did you always want to swim with the cute pigs from the Bahamas? This and way more dreams can become true once you decide to book a flight to this exciting destination.

Blue Lagoon Island

This private island, only 5km from Nassau, is a real paradise we found. Your chances are good that you will see dolphins and sealions in their natural habitat while you are taking a well-deserved break at the beach. And if you are up for a thrill board a double-decker catamaran and sail next to the historic harbour or explore new islands.

Exuma Cayes

You cannot leave the Bahamas without paying the pigs at Big Major Cay, also called Pig Beach, a visit. So book a boat safari! And after this adventure join the Staniel Cay Yacht Club for a lunch including fresh sea food.

Ideal Travel Times

Visit Nassau from November to April to skip the hurricane season and enjoy your spring break.
